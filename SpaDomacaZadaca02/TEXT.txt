Welcome to Conway's Game of Life.
You can move using just your mouse's ScrollWheel, or by holding Crtl or Shift and using the ScrollWheel.
You can place dots by LeftClicking or remove them by RightClicking.
You can press R to reset your view, and M to load the info screen.
You can use Left and Right arrows to control processing time. Space is used to pause/unpause the simulation.
Up and Down arrows control how many chunks regeneration will create. You can trigger it by pressing BackSpace.
Hitting Enter will display chunk data.
Escape ends the program.

Tap space to begin.
